Description

Built in 2020, Exotic is a website that promotes and makes easy access to foreign foods. Our focus is on restaurants, food lovers, tourists and people who like exotic food. Exotic wants to be a platform that gathers people around food. On exotic.io, you can search food by country, culture and name.

Mission

  • Promote and make easy access to exotic food.
  • Gather people around food.
  • Centralize the sale of exotic food.
  • Encourage people to discover other culture food.
